Miya: A Sweet Soul with Mysterious Mind

Miya is a creative spirit thriving in the heart of Atlanta, Georgia, a city rich in diversity and inspiration. She loves exploring new places, especially her beloved "Nations Market," where cultures blend in a vibrant mix of flavors and stories. With an eye for all things charming, she adores pretty packaging, miniatures, and dreamy home décor—especially in pink!

A natural visionary, Miya finds joy in bringing her ideas to life, whether through DIY projects, baking, or design. She balances her soft, whimsical aesthetic with a fascination for the eerie and mysterious, drawn to psychological thrillers, horror films, and true crime podcasts while still appreciating the warmth of romantic comedies and Disney classics.

Beyond her passions, Miya is deeply intentional about how she lives, finding beauty in both the little moments and the bigger picture. Though introverted, she has a bubbly and uplifting presence, making conversations feel effortless and refreshing. She listens intently, encourages others, and fills every space she enters with joy, creativity, and a touch of magic. If she could have any superpower, she’d love to be psychic—not just to see what’s ahead, but to sharpen her intuition (and maybe even win the lottery!).

Things Miya Enjoys

Miya finds joy in creativity, discovery, and a touch of the unexpected. Whether she’s crafting, baking, or diving into a good thriller, she embraces every moment with passion.
